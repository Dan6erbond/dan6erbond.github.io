Substarters is a Discord-based Reddit community by Redditors for Redditors. Its main purpose being to help new and old subreddits get their feet off the ground as well as help people moderate subreddits.

The members of the community help each other out by delivering tips on topics ranging from moderation to initial setup, suggestions on rules - anything you can think of. These tips don't only come from the Substarters team and Astronauts either! Everyone is part of the community and to encourage helping out, the team has implemented Starma.

ServerðŸ”—

Currently the server has around 150 members, of which around 50 of them are signed up as Helpers. We've had over 40 subreddits join us so far and some of them are now running on a fully autonomous community and show admirable activity.

In addition there are many people with previous experience in moderating subreddits as well as tons of newcomers, so as in the true Substarters spirit, we have a wide varietey of community members and are a very active community.

  • Subreddits: Here every subreddits gets their very own channel where any help they need can be discussed and roles are pinged to get as many community members onboard the process as possible.

BotsðŸ”—

Substarters makes lots of use of Discord as well as Reddit bots to aid new moderators in getting their community setup. Alongside using the Mariavi bots Reddify and Melon, they have two custom bots designed for particularly this community.

SubstarðŸ”—

Substar was the first bot created for the community and has helped guide the users through the basic processes, especially as it used to hold Tags which have since been rebranded to Melons and delivered lots of general information.

The bot has changed a lot with the founding of Mariavi and now guides users through the verification and introduction process as well as organizes the list of Subreddits in the Discord server to ensure an organized community.

There are a couple useful commands Substar offers in addition to the automated processes and he also runs Starma!

!addsub will guide you through the process of creating a channel for your subreddit. It will ask you some general questions so it can ping roles and suggest commands once the channel has been created as well as determine whether your subreddit is eligible:

Once your channel has been created, many things can be done! One of the more recent features added to Substar is the command !css. This command will apply a modified version of the Naut theme, we like to call Astronaut. The bot will needed to be added as a moderator to the subreddit in order for this feature to work, but it will unmod itself immediately after applying the theme. The roadmap for this command includes a color picker to allow you to pick the accent and main colors and maybe a dedicated dark theme.

SubmoonðŸ”—

Submoon is our advertisement bot that doesn't interact too much with the actual Substarters community. She uses a couple algorithms to scan through newly created subreddits as well as advertisement-based subreddits to find eligible members and streams them to a channel only the team has access to. After an authorization by a singular team member the bot will send an invite and after that it's all up to Substar to retain the users.

In addition to finding eligible members, Submoon makes use of the scan to find subreddits that may have potential, but lost their moderator team and backing.
