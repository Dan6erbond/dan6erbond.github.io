About usðŸ”—

The members are Amy and Ravi who both met each other through Reddit. Ravi saw an ad she put up for people interested in moderating her subreddit and volunteered. They quickly became friends and started chatting about all their ideas such as a bot that would let them moderate the subreddit through Discord. A concept quickly became reality and soon enough, the subreddit was practically being run through Discord. It would allow the mod-team to vote on things easier and get pinged through reports and new posts. They were able to step up their moderation game quite a bit.

After the subreddit's moderator team dissolved, Amy and Ravi thought about creating a more customizeable version of the Discord moderation bot which they called Banhammer. A couple weeks into the project security flaws were discovered and the project was put to a halt and Reddify was born to ensure a secure connection between Discord and Reddit via 2FA. As they took on more projects such as revamping Substarters they decided their digital friendship needed a name, Mariavi was born.

They have a Discord server which you are always welcome to join and Amy has a website of her own! Check it out!

Banhammer

The Banhammer Bot is a Discord bot, currently in development, that allows Reddit moderators to moderate their subreddit from within Discord.
It has been inspired by the concept of the bot we used to use back at /r/casualChildAbuse and is very simple to setup! Simply invite it to your Discord server and use !addsub to add your subreddit to it's database.

Important: /u/TheBanhammerBot needs to be a moderator of your subreddit in order to function properly.

Reddify

Reddify is a Discord bot, that intends to connect your Reddit account with your Discord one via 2FA. Simply use !verify to have it send you a message and respond with "verify" to the message in Reddit and Reddify will take care of the rest!

Server admins can use !toggle [nick/role] to disable or enable the nickname and role feature on Reddify which changes the user's nickname (Reddit username)/role ('VERIFIED REDDITOR').

Melon

Melon is the first Discord bot to officially use Fuzzle. She is capable of storing "Melons" categorized by the devs that can be summoned by typing !melon [search]. She will then find the closest matches and if there isn't a 100% match, offer you to select one of them with reactions.

Other handy feature include reaction-roles: use !addreactrole [emoji, role, msg, channel] to have her add a reaction to a message that allows users to sign-up for roles in that server!

SMART

Staxel Modding and Related Tools is a GUI application built on the JavaFX framework for players and modders of the game Staxel that enhances the experience of creating mods as well as playing the game.

Currently it features a Lang builder as well as a mod builder for .tile files, but more is in the works!
