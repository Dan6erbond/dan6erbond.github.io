About usðŸ”—

The members are Amy and Ravi who both met each other through Reddit. Ravi saw an ad she put up for people interested in moderating her subreddit and volunteered. They quickly became friends and started chatting about all their ideas such as a bot that would let them moderate the subreddit through Discord. A concept quickly became reality and soon enough, the subreddit was practically being run through Discord. It would allow the mod-team to vote on things easier and get pinged through reports and new posts. They were able to step up their moderation game quite a bit.

After the subreddit's moderator team dissolved, Amy and Ravi thought about creating a more customizeable version of the Discord moderation bot which they called Banhammer. A couple weeks into the project security flaws were discovered and the project was put to a halt and Reddify was born to ensure a secure connection between Discord and Reddit via 2FA. As they took on more projects such as revamping Substarters they decided their digital friendship needed a name, Mariavi was born.

They have a Discord server which you are always welcome to join and Amy has a website of her own! Check it out!

ProjectsðŸ”—

Banhammer

The Banhammer Bot is a Discord bot, currently in development, that allows Reddit moderators to moderate their subreddit from within Discord.

It makes use of the Banhammer.py framework and requires zero coding experience to be setup in your own Discord server!

Wiki Discord

Banhammer.py

Banhammer.py is a framework built for the Discord.py Discord API Wrapper that enables you to create your very own Banhammer!

By working with the asyncio event loop it will stream new and reported items to your bot as well as handle the reactions.

Discord

Reddify

Reddify is a Discord bot, that intends to connect your Reddit account with your Discord one via 2FA. Once you've verified your Reddit account once, you won't have to do it again! Every server will now know you and apply the configured settings.

Melon

Melon is the first Discord bot to officially use Fuzzle. She's the ideal server utility bot with features such as reaction-roles, Automoji, poll support and of course - Melons.

Melons are tidbits of information that can be searched through by using !melon [search].
