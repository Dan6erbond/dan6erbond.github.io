SubstarðŸ”—

Status

Substar was the first bot created for the community and has helped guide the users through the basic processes, especially as it used to hold Tags which have since been rebranded to Melons and delivered lots of general information.

The bot has changed a lot with the founding of Mariavi and now guides users through the verification and introduction process as well as organizes the list of Subreddits in the Discord server to ensure an organized community.

There are a couple useful commands Substar offers in addition to the automated processes and he also runs Starma!

!addsub will guide you through the process of creating a channel for your subreddit. It will ask you some general questions so it can ping roles and suggest commands once the channel has been created as well as determine whether your subreddit is eligible:

!cssðŸ”—

One of the more recent features added to Substar is the command !css. This command will apply a modified version of the Naut theme, we like to call Astronaut. The bot will needed to be added as a moderator to the subreddit in order for this feature to work, but it will unmod itself immediately after applying the theme. The roadmap for this command includes a color picker to allow you to pick the accent and main colors and maybe a dedicated dark theme.

This theme will be applied to the subreddit for which the command is being ran. A live preview can be seen here.

Subreddits with potentialðŸ”—

Submoon is our advertisement bot that doesn't interact too much with the actual Substarters community. She uses a couple algorithms to scan through newly created subreddits as well as advertisement-based subreddits to find eligible members and streams them to a channel only the team has access to.

Similarly to Banhammer, Submoon makes use of reactions and adds a couple of them to each subreddit found. Once a member of the Substarters team clicks on any of the reactions, the subreddit will either be invited, disregarded or reported as an unmarked NSFW sub, all with the goal of making Reddit a better place:

With the use of reactions the Substarters team can invite subreddits and give subreddits not marked as NSFW a heads-up.
